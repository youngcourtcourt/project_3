What's it all about, anyway?



Using the depth, magnitude, Sensor's Distance to Epicenter, Azimuthal gap, and Root mean Square, a Support Vector Machine will predict the predominant seismic waveform of the earthquake event

And what do those charts mean?


The pie chart depicts the distribution of wave forms within the training data. As for the scatter plot, the green dots represent accurate predictions and the red X's represent incorrect. The conclusion is that our model is not adept at predicting 3.0-4.0 waveforms or any greater than ~5.5.
